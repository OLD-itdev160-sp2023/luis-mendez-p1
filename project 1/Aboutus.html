About Us

Café Latte has been serving the community for over 10 years. Our mission is to provide the highest quality coffee, pastries, and sandwiches in a cozy and welcoming atmosphere.

Our coffee is locally sourced and roasted in small batches to ensure freshness and quality. Our pastries are made in-house every day, using only the finest ingredients.

We are a family-owned business, and we take pride in serving our community. We believe that a great cup of coffee can bring people together, and we strive to create an environment where people can relax, unwind, and connect.
